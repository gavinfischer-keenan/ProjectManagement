# Tutorial: How to Create Your First Task and Sections

Welcome to **Hawaii PM v1.0.0**! This guide will walk you through setting up your first project tasks and organizing them into sections.

## Overview
Hawaii PM is designed to help you track complex projects (like construction or maintenance) by grouping tasks into logical "Sections" (e.g., Electrical, Plumbing, Permitting) and then adding individual "Tasks" under those sections.

---

## Step 1: Create a Section
Sections are the high-level categories for your work. They help keep your dashboard and project tracker organized.

1. Navigate to the **Project Tracker** tab using the top navigation bar.
2. In the sidebar on the left side of the screen, you'll see a button labeled **+ Section**. Click it.
3. A form will appear. 
   - **Name:** Enter a descriptive name for this phase or category (e.g., "Main House Roof" or "Permits").
   - **Order:** You can assign a number to dictate its position on the dashboard and tracker (e.g., `1` for the first section, `2` for the second, etc.).
4. Click **Save**. You will now see your new section appear in the main tracker view and on your Dashboard!

---

## Step 2: Add a Task to Your Section
Now that you have a section, it’s time to populate it with actionable tasks.

1. On the **Project Tracker** page, find the Section you just created.
2. Click the **+ Add Task** button located below the section name.
3. The Task creation form will open. Here is a breakdown of the key fields:
   - **Title:** The name of the task (e.g., "Order Roofing Materials").
   - **Owner:** Who is responsible for this?
   - **Status:** Starts as `Not Started`, but you can update it to `In Progress`, `Blocked`, or `Completed`.
   - **Timeline:** Set a Start Date and End Date. These dates will automatically populate the Gantt Chart view!
4. **Important Checkboxes:**
   - **Milestone:** Checking this box automatically logs the task to the **Maintenance Log** (Milestones tab) once it's created or updated, creating a permanent record of major project events.
   - **Hardware:** Checking this box automatically logs the task to the **Maintenance Log** (Hardware tab), perfect for tracking installations or equipment orders.
5. Click **Save**. Your task will now appear under its parent section.

---

## Step 3: Update and Track Progress
As work gets done, you'll want to update the task to reflect reality.

1. In the **Project Tracker**, simply click on any task row to open the details panel.
2. **Add Updates:** Use the **Add Update** field at the bottom of the task panel to add date-stamped notes on your progress.
3. **Change Status:** Move the task to `In Progress` or `Completed`.
4. **Watch the Dashboard:** Navigate back to the **Summary Dashboard** tab. You'll see the progress bar for your Section automatically calculate the completion percentage based on the statuses of its tasks.

---

## Pro-Tips
- **Dashboard Navigation:** Did you know you can click on any section name in the Summary Dashboard to instantly jump to that section in the Project Tracker?
- **Gantt Timeline:** Switch to the Gantt view to visualize your schedule. You can click on the task names on the left side of the Gantt chart to instantly open their details and make quick edits.

**You're all set! Enjoy managing your projects with Hawaii PM.**
